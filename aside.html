Doggy Walking Hints and Suggestions!

Walking a puppy or dog is important for both owner and pet. You do not need a garden or a park. As long as you are willing to walk, walking by the local shops helps to socialize your pet and accustom him or her to their environment!

Cost Saving Food!

Always a good idea to recycle left over food, that is not too spicy, it is great way to save money.

Quick and Easy Ingrediants!

I use eggs, stale bread, white boiled rice and raw meat to create tasty dog food. Dogs recognize their own items like their food bowls, so donate an old large one! I love to watch my dog eat tasty food, it goes down really quickly!
